Meet Alex

Alex halpern

Massage Therapist

I trained at the Oregon School of Massage, and gravitated toward the Eastern philosophy of Shiatsu. Shiatsu is a Japanese form of bodywork based on the holistic system of Traditional Chinese Medicine that uses finger pressure and movement to improve circulation, loosen stiff muscles, and strengthen mental well being.

It is my goal to not only relieve your pain, but also to rewaken your connection to your body.

I keep my offerings affordable and convenient (I will come to you!) It is my hope that in doing so, bodywork becomes more accessible - as a regular part of a holistic health routine.
